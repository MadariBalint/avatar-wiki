The _**Bush Boss FD-11**_ or the **Flamer Pistol** is a heavy flamethrower that was designed to be used with [AMP suits](/amp). It is comparably lighter than the [[GAU-90]], which means that the AMP suit could wield it with one hand if needed.
[[AMP Pyros]] and [[Mercer's Guard AMP]]s often dual-wield them, and they have additional fuel tanks strapped to their arms for additional ammo at the expanse of creating a weak point.

## Description

It is aesthetically similar to the [[Bush Boss FD-3]], but considerably upscaled to accommodate the AMP suit. The weapon is 1.82 meters long and has a dry weight of 90.7 kilograms. The FD-11 is mainly used for deforestation and perimeter patrols around [[Hell's Gate]].
