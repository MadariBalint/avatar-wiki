_**The World of Avatar: A Visual Exploration**_ is a book that visually explores locations, creatures, technology, and other "key content" from the Avatar franchise. While similar in focus to _[[James Cameron's Avatar: An Activist Survival Guide]]_, the book is the first that includes material released after the first Avatar film, specifically Toruk - The First Flight and Pandora: The World of Avatar.

An updated version, _[[The World of Avatar]]: Updated Edition_, was released in November 2025 ahead of _[[Avatar: Fire and Ash]]_.
