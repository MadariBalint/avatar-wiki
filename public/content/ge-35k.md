The _**GE-35K**_ or more commonly known as the **AMP Grenade Launchers** are a pair of snub-barrelled incendiary grenade launchers used by the [[RDA]] in the [[Western Frontier]]. They are only found used by the [[AMP Grenadiers]] and the [[Mercer's Guard AMPs]].

## Description

Whilst it bares superficial similarities to the Chunker, the AMP Grenade Launchers differ in that it is more situated for ground-based targets than sea-based ones.

They are a pair of modified snub-barrelled automatic grenade launchers with reinforced armoured panels that could launch a dozen grenades at quick succession. The rapid firing rate is compensated by a large ammo belt connecting the twin-linked weapons together. The grenades it fires are incendiary high-explosives, allowing for a rain of devastating explosions that shower the area in a hail of fragmentation and fire. This makes it incredibly lethal but also prone to collateral damage as the indiscriminate firing will destroy anything within its blast radius.

Another feature is that the grenades are timed rather than impact-based. This is to minimize potential friendly-fire accidents. As the AMP Grenadiers fire them in an upward trajectory, they behave more like mortars than grenade launchers, acting as large artillery pieces in the process.
