_**Avatar: Adapt or Die**_ is a six-part comic series being published by Dark Horse Comics. It is a prequel to the first film, following [[Grace Augustine]]'s attempts to bridge a peace with the [[Omatikaya clan]].

The first issue was released on May 11, 2022, after seeing a week-long delay. The next five issues were released the same year on June 1, July 6, August 3, September 7, and October 5. A trade paperback combining all six issues released on March 21, 2023.

It is unclear when exactly the comic is set, but it is most likely the late 2130s to early 2140s, but highly unlikely it is set past 2142. Grace has not opened the schoolhouse yet in the comic, and she mentions she poured a decade of her life into the school during the movie, while the schoolhouse was closed in 2152.
