The **Fan Lizard** (Na’vi name: **Kenten**; pronunciation: [ˈkɛn.tɛn]) is a small reptilian animal found in the rainforests of [[Pandora]].

## Description

At first glance, the fan lizard appears to be rather dull, with unremarkable brownish skin and body plan. However, when startled, it unfurls a large fan-like membrane from its back, which allows it to glide through the air away from the disturbance. This membrane gives the lizard its common [[human]] name.

## Anatomy

Fan lizards are known for the large retractable membrane on their back, which is similar in structure to the Da Vinci helicopter. This membrane is yellow during the day, but glows with bioluminescence in many different colours at night. It is used for flight as well as to ward off predators with the sudden increase in size and display of light. Flight with the membrane causes the fan lizard’s body to spin rapidly, and so is generally only used for short periods.

## Feeding ecology

The fan lizard is an omnivore that feeds on tree sap and small insects. It is also nocturnal, as many of these insects are active at night.
