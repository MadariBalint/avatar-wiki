_**The World of Avatar: Updated Edition**_ was announced on April 30, 2025 by DK. It was released on November 4, 2025.
It is an updated version of _[[The World of Avatar: A Visual Exploration]]_, featuring material from _[[Avatar: The Way of Water]]_, _[[Avatar: Fire and Ash]]_, _[[Avatar: Frontiers of Pandora]]_, and even the cancelled _[[Avatar: Reckoning]]_.
