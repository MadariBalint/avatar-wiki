**Dawn fruit** is a fruit which grows a hard shell that needs to be broken by force in order to reach the juicy core. A close relation of [[shell fruit]], dawn fruit grows on a creeping vine which favors vertical surfaces, often artificial walls.
Beneath its shell is a pink, slightly sour flesh.

It is most commonly found in abandoned [[RDA]] facilities throughout [[Pandora]]. Rarer dawn fruits are often found in elevated areas in overgrown RDA facilities.
