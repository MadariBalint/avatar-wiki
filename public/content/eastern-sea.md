The **Eastern Sea** is one of the seas on [[Pandora]]. It is first seen in _[[Avatar]]_ briefly when [[Jake Sully]] and [[Neytiri]] visit the [[Tayrangi clan[[.

The Sully family later moves across the Eastern Sea to seek _uturu_ with the [[Metkayina clan]]. It is said the Metkayina atolls are just under 300 miles (483km) north-east to where the [Omatikaya](/omatikaya-clan) live, and it takes about 5 hours of [[ikran]] flying (excluding rest stops).
