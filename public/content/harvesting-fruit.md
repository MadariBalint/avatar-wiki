The **harvesting fruits** are ginormous fruits used as food sources by the [[Na'vi]].

## Description

Like much of the flora and fauna on [[Pandora]], fruits and vegetables can potentially grow up to colossal size. Giant fruit growing on a variety of trees near Hometree provide a valuable source of food for the Na'vi. The fruit is dissected to be eaten immediately or packaged in large leaves for distribution and/or storage.
