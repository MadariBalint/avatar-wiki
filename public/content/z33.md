The _**Z-33 (Zarkov-33) Pistol**_ is the new standard-issue sidearm of the [[RDA]], replacing the older RDA Handgun. It is carried by most SecOps personnel, including General Ardmore and [[Cet-Ops]] members.
An enlarged version, known as the **Z-33R**, also serves as the standard sidearm for [[Skel Suit]] operators and [[Recombinant]]s.

## Description

The Z-33 uses a .40 caliber round, while the Z-33R fires 0226-LSAR (Linkable Skel Assault Round) hollow-point ammunition designed to fragment on impact, causing severe lacerations and extensive internal damage to living targets.

The Z-33R can fire up to 40 rounds per minute, making it an extremely lethal sidearm at close range. It can hold up to 16 rounds per magazine.
