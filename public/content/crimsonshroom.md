The **Crimson Mushroom** grows in shades of pink and purple and contains a pigmented xylem which gives it a distinctive crimson head. It is found only on high vines in the [[Kinglor Forest]].
Crimson mushrooms are most commonly found on large roots and great vines throughout the Kinglor Forest.

Rarer crimson mushrooms are often found in the Swamp Lowlands biome of the Kinglor Forest.

It is best gathered at night.
