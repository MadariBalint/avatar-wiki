**Vineshroom** is a type of mushroom in _[[Avatar: Frontiers of Pandora]]_ most commonly found hanging below floating mountains throughout Pandora. Rarer vineshrooms are often found at higher altitudes on floating mountains. Best quality vineshrooms can be harvested at night.

Protruding from a hanging stem, the vineshroom grows attached to the giant vine trees and floating rock formations. It has a watery consistency and is often dried before cooking. Using a vineshroom as the main cooking ingredient in combination with bone helm rhino meat is rumored to have a special effect.
