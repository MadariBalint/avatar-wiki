_**Avatar: Frontiers of Pandora — So'lek's Journey**_ is a six-issue comic mini-series published by Dark Horse Comics. It is written by Ray Fawkes, and illustrated by Gabriel Guzman and Michael Atiyeh.

It follows the journey of [[So'lek]], a [[Na'vi]] character who also appears in _[[Avatar: Frontiers of Pandora]]_; the comic is a tie-in to the game.

The first issue was released on February 28, 2024. Issues 5 and 6 were delayed; Issue 5 was originally planned for June 26, 2024, but was delayed to July 17 2024. Issue 6 was planned for July 31, 2024, but it was first delayed to August 21, 2024, and then again to September 18, 2024.

A trade paperback including all six issues of the comic was going to be released on December 17, 2024, however it was subsequently delayed to January 7, 2025.
