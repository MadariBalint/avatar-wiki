The _**AMP Submachine Gun**_ is a stripped down and smaller version of the larger [[GAU-90]] cannons used by the [[RDA]] in the [[Western Frontier]]. Two variants are used by the [[AMP Grenadier]]s and [[Combat Exos]], although they both function the same.

## Description

Nothing more than a one-handed GAU-90, the AMP Submachine Gun is still considered a heavy weapon when placed in the hands of a regular human. Nevertheless, this stripped down weapon is designed for two goals. The first is that it allows smaller frames such as the Combat Exo to reliably wield them with one hand whilst still boasting similar firepower of the GAU-90. The second is that it provides good close-combat support for the AMP Grenadiers when enemies gets too close for comfort.

Like the GAU-90, the AMP Submachine Gun is chambered for 30mm munitions, and is capable of cutting down all but the largest beasts of [[Pandora]]. The weapon's magazine is placed on the top of the gun and it lacks the ammo belt of its larger cousin, meaning that the user must manually reload the weapon once the ammo has been used up.

As aforementioned, there are two variants of this weapon. There is the standard variant used by Combat Exos and than there is the AMP Grenadier variants which is more reinforced, has a radically different design and uses a unique helical magazine rather than a top-load magazine.
