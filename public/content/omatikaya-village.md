**Omatikaya Village** is a temporary Na'vi village inhabited by the [[Omatikaya clan]] around 2154-2168. It was built by the survivors of the Destruction of the Hometree in the tropical forest near [[Hell's Gate]], inhabited by the humans allied to the Na'vi since they banished the [[RDA]] from [[Pandora]].

## Description

As a place of temporary residence, the village lacked a uniform and closed structure. Shops, maruis, and canopies stretched just outside the Hell's Gate fence; some Na'vi have also adapted the Avatar Compound as an enclosed place suited to the residence and activity of individuals of their size and stature. The location of the village was not chosen by chance, because Jake Sully wanted the Na'vi and humans to start living together symbolically, to rebuild what had been destroyed during the war.

Much of the town stretched deep into the forest, where the Omatikaya began to live as a clan without the closed, clustered home of Hometree, that is, in a more dispersed manner. The Na'vi live in maruis spread out among the trees, presumably separated for each family.
