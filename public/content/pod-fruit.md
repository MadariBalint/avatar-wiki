**Pod Fruit** is flora and a cooking ingredient in _[[Avatar: Frontiers of Pandora]]_.
Pod fruit is the berry of the [[Sheltering Bushpod]]. Rarer pod fruits can be found in the Wild Purple Steppe biome of the [[Upper Plains]]. Best-quality pod fruit can be harvested in rainy weather.

Pod fruits are a staple food of the [[Zeswa Clan]]. The mildly sweet pulp is cooked and eaten in many formats and is traditionally used to make teas and cordials. Using pod fruit as the main cooking ingredient in combination with soundblast colossus fatty meat is rumoured to have a special effect.
