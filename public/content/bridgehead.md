_**Bridgehead**_, also known as **Bridgehead City**, is a massive, city-like base established by the [Resources Development Administration](/rda) near the Pandoran oceans.

Meant to save humanity from extinction due to Earth dying from pollution and overpopulation, Bridgehead serves as the successor to [[Hell's Gate]]. The city is ground zero for human colonization of Pandora and the RDA's aquatic operations (Cetacean Operations).

It was built by AI and 3D printing, resulting in decades worth of manual labor being finished in only a year. Despite its large size, it currently only has a population of around 2,500 people. However, the RDA intends to reach a population of 2 million eventually, whether it be ferrying people from Earth during its mass evacuation, as well as having Bridgehead residents reproduce over generations within the city into the 23rd century and beyond.
