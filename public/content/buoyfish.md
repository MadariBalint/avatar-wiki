**Buoyfish** (Na'vi name: **lìngpay**) are small fish found in waterways in the [[Western Frontier]].

Found across the Western Frontier, buoyfish are often spotted at the bottom of rivers feeding on small fish and critters on the riverbed. They can do this for prolonged periods of time thanks to a pair of inflatable bladders that sit above the fish itself. With these, the fish can suspend themselves in place and remain stable as they feed. Their bladders are also used as part of a display during the buoyfish's mating ritual.

The fish itself has a large, colorful carapace that is sectioned across the body, allowing for flexible movement while serving as a defense against predators. The buoyfish also has four pairs of strong fins that allow it to move at impressive speeds through the water, rapidly changing directions.
