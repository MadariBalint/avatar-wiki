The **Nightwraith** (Na’vi name: **Ska’avum**; pronunciation: [skaʔ.a.vum]) is a rare, apex aerial predator on [[Pandora]].

## Anatomy

The nightwraith shares many characteristics with the [[tetrapteron]] - and is a distant relative of it - but is uncharacteristically large enough to warrant a species delineation. It has four wings, each with ten folding vanes that splay out and provide a more precise level of aerial control than an [[ikran]]. It has a hollow bony crest on its head, known as a casque. It is known to use its casque to spear the operculum of its prey when hunting, for defense against predators, and in mating rituals. Its talons are similarly deadly, and can be used to strike down aerial prey, much like a Terran hawk or falcon.
